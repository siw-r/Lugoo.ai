About Lugoo

Lugoo is an AI-powered Chatbot designed to demystify the complexities of immigration, with a particular focus on asylum-related legal data. Derived from the Arabic لجؤ—meaning asylum or refuge—Lugoo serves as a bridge between institutional legal frameworks and the urgent need for accessible, structured guidance. By leveraging cutting-edge natural language processing, it synthesizes the latest asylum laws and procedural updates, equipping applicants with precise, up-to-date information to navigate their paths with ease and clarity. Initially developed as a project in response to bureaucratic opacity, Lugoo aims to be an indispensable tool for enabling informed decision-making and a catalyst for meaningful integration into German society.
